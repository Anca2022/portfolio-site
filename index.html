About me

I am a JavaScript developer specializing in the Angular frontend framework, with a focus on responsive design and accessibility. My unique background in medicine has instilled in me a strong sense of discipline, promoting continuous skill development and adaptability to diverse working environments and team dynamics, traits that I bring into my work as a developer. I am eager to collaborate on projects as an integral part of a team.

In my spare time, I enjoy spending quality time with friends, celebrating the bonds that enrich my life, trying new cuisines at local restaurant (I am a foodie at heart), reading nonfiction books and nurturing my growing plant collection.

This project aims to provide a user-friendly platform for a car rental firm based in Cluj-Napoca.
